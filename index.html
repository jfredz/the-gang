Once upon a time there was a group of shapes. They were mostly black and rectangular. They were mean and very serious.

And so they kicked the 3 shapes that did not conform out of their gang, which was called the right Angled Gang.

These 3 shapes were very sad, but found great joy and confort being around each other. They soon started having a lot of fun, started rocking and rolling, and doing drugs.

They even started making fun of rectangular shapes calling them "too square"! This way, they became the most well-known gang in the universe.
